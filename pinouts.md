
RJ45 connection pinout:
 1 - Manual Open
 2 - Manual Close
 3 - Mode Select
 4 - Reset
 5 - Activity LED
 6 - Power LED
 7 - not in use
 8 - Common/Ground



ESP32 pinout:
 2  - power led
 19 - activity led
 14 - stepper driver sleep (slp)
 27 - stepper driver step (stp)
 26 - stepper driver dir (dir)
 25 - mode_switch (auto/manual)
 32 - close_limit
 33 - open_limit
 35 - obstruction_limit
 15 - manual_open
 4  - manual_close
